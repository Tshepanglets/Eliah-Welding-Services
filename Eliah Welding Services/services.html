Our Services

At Eliah Welding Services, we offer a comprehensive range of welding solutions and welding techniques. We Design, build and install: Regulation Fire Escapes; Balustrades; Juliet Balconies; Metal Gates; etc. Our services extend to structural welding, custom fabrication, and on-site repairs. With a focus on safety and efficiency, we ensure exceptional results every time. We also work with steel; stainless steel; mid steel; brass; copper; bronze; etc.

What We Do

Fabricating

As a premier welding service provider, we specialize in fabricating custom metalwork to the highest standards, ensuring precision and adherence to client specifications.

Sawing

We utilize powerful technology to ensure precise and accurate cuts on any metal surface through our sawing services, providing a comprehensive welding experience.

Fusing

As a versatile welding provider, we offer techniques including fusing, which ensures a high-quality, seamless bonding on all metal surfaces.

More Services

Glass Embedding

Our glass embedding services transform any project into a masterpiece by seamlessly incorporating glass into metal surfaces, utilizing expert skill to create a stunning visual effect.

Stone Setting

Our stone setting services offer the opportunity to seamlessly integrate beautiful stones into your metalwork, creating unique and eye-catching pieces with the expert skill of our welders.

Timber Inlaying

We also work with timber, offering specialized inlaying and custom designs to complement our high-quality welding services seamlessly and efficiently, ensuring exceptional craftsmanship and durability.
